Consumption-based IT on your terms 
 
Do you need to deliver outcomes to your business faster and with less 
risk?  
Do you want to pay for what you use, instead of what you might use?  
Are you ready for a simpler way to consume IT?  
 
Whether it’s real-time analytics, connecting devices at the edge, or 
protecting apps and data, business leaders now widely prefer to 
consume the outcomes they want, when they want them, and pay 
only for what they consume.  
 
Join consumption economics expert, JB Wood, and HPE’s Craig 
Routledge, to learn about the latest trends in consumption-based IT 
outcomes, including HPE GreenLake.
